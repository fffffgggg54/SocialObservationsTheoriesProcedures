

Class 0 - No relationship present. Two people have not interacted outside of those as defined by societal conventions.
          Typically people who pass each other on the street, people in the same area and haven't interacted beyond smalltalk.
          
Class 1 - A relationship between two people who are familiar with each other and have interacted a few times.
          Identifiers and other personal details are typically known. Communication is typically sought.
          Similar to an aquaintance.
